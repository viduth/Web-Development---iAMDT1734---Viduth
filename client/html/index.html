Our Story

In 2005, two Artrepreneurs decided it was time to prove everyone who thought that Creative people cannot have successful careers, wrong. And the ‘AMDT School of Creativity’ was born.

Since then, AMDT School of Creativity has been relentless in inspiring Creative minds and brought relief to countless parents who were worried that their Creative child might not have a successful career in their chosen Creative disciplines.

Our Creative vision

'AMDT School of Creativity' is the pioneering institute for Creativity in Sri Lanka. Our Creative vision is to make Sri Lanka a Creative country. We invite everyone to support us in our journey to make Sri Lanka Creative.
